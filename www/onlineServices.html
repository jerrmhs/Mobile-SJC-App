Reporting Instructions

As call-in jurors you are required to check this website beginning at 5:00 p.m. as indicated on your jury summons. Updated instructions will be posted on this website daily. If you are not instructed to report by the end of the week following your summons date, your service will be completed.

If employed, please check this web site from work. DO NOT take the day off from work. If you are not required to report, you simply stay at work and follow the instructions posted.

Being on "stand-by" or "on-call" jury service may not suit your schedule. If this is the case, please call (209) 992-5500 so that jury staff can work with your schedule to arrange for a specific date for you to report for jury service.

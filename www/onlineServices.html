Reporting Instructions

As call-in jurors you are required to check this website beginning at 5:00 p.m. as indicated on your jury summons. Updated instructions will be posted on this website daily. If you are not instructed to report by the end of the week following your summons date, your service will be completed.

If employed, please check this web site from work. DO NOT take the day off from work. If you are not required to report, you simply stay at work and follow the instructions posted.

Being on "stand-by" or "on-call" jury service may not suit your schedule. If this is the case, please call (209) 992-5500 so that jury staff can work with your schedule to arrange for a specific date for you to report for jury service.

To see the Jury Status please connect to the internet to view the resuilts.

 

AT THIS TIME, JURY TRIALS ONLY TAKE PLACE IN STOCKTON. ALL JURORS INSTRUCTED TO REPORT FOR JURY SERVICE SHOULD CHECK-IN WITH THE JURY ASSEMBLY ROOM LOCATED IN THE BASEMENT OF THE STOCKTON COURTHOUSE AT 222 E. WEBER AVENUE, STOCKTON, CA 95202. Please bring your entire summons with you and have the residence phone number, work number, occupation and employer information filled in before you come into the jury assembly room. Fill in the name and address information only if you have a change of name and/or address. Make sure to sign the juror information form before you turn it in to the jury staff.

Designated handicapped parking spaces are available adjacent to the Courthouse on Weber, San Joaquin and Main Streets, as well as the Essential Services Building.

If you require other disability related parking accommodations, please telephone the jury staff at 992-5500 and advise them of your need.
